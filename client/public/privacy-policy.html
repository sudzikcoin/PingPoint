Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 6, 2026

PingPoint Driver ("the App") is operated by Suverse ("we", "us", or "our"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

Location Data: When you enable GPS sharing, we collect your real-time geographic location. Location sharing is optional and can be turned on or off at any time.

Load and Activity Data: We collect information related to your assigned loads, including stop arrivals, departures, timestamps, and completion status.

Device Information: We may collect basic device information such as device type, operating system version, and app version.

PingPoints Data: We track your earned reward points within the App.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Share your real-time location with your assigned broker/dispatcher
  • Display your load assignment details and stop information
  • Track stop arrivals and departures
  • Calculate and display your PingPoints reward balance
  • Improve the App performance and user experience

3. Location Data

When GPS sharing is enabled, your coordinates are sent to the PingPoint server at regular intervals so your dispatcher can see your position. You have full control and can disable it at any time. When off, no GPS data is collected.

4. Data Sharing

We share your information only with your assigned broker/dispatcher and service providers for hosting. We do not sell your data or share it with advertisers.

5. Data Retention

We retain data for as long as necessary to provide our services. Location data is retained only for real-time tracking duration.

6. Data Security

We use encrypted transmission (HTTPS/TLS), secure infrastructure, and access controls to protect your data.

7. Your Rights

  • Enable or disable location sharing at any time
  • Request information about the data we hold about you
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Withdraw consent for data processing

8. Childrens Privacy

PingPoint Driver is intended for professional truck drivers and is not directed at children under 13.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy and will update the Last updated date above.
